About PJPL

The Professional Junior Pickleball League (PJPL) was founded in 2023 to showcase top junior talent across the globe. Our mission is to foster sportsmanship, development, and community engagement through high-level competition.

In just two seasons, the PJPL has grown to include eight elite teams, featuring rising stars like Carsten Sheuer and Caje Riess. We’re proud to offer an inclusive environment where every athlete can sharpen their skills and inspire the next generation of players.
